Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you decide.

How much does automation cost?

Pricing depends on complexity and scope. Simple workflows take 1-2 weeks to implement. Complex integrations may take 4-6 weeks. We provide a detailed, customized quote after the free discovery audit. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

How long does implementation take?

Most projects complete in 2-4 weeks. Simple workflows (single-tool automation) take 1-2 weeks. Complex integrations with multiple systems may take 4-6 weeks. We work with your schedule and provide a timeline after the discovery audit.

Which tools can you integrate?

We integrate with HubSpot, Odoo, Salesforce, Google Sheets, Gmail, WhatsApp, Zapier, Make, Airtable, and most major CRMs and business tools. If your tool has an API, we can connect it. Custom integrations are available for enterprise clients.

Do you replace my team or enhance them?

We enhance. AI handles repetitive work (data entry, basic support, lead follow-up) so your team can focus on strategy, creativity, and complex problem-solving. You don't hire fewer people — you get more done with the same team.

What if we need to change or expand automation later?

Our system is built to scale. Adding new workflows or expanding existing ones is straightforward. We provide comprehensive support post-launch and can help you expand automation as your business grows. Contact us for details on ongoing support options.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use enterprise-grade encryption, secure API connections, and follow data protection best practices. Your data never leaves your systems — agents only read/write what you authorize. We comply with Kenya's Data Protection Act (DPA 2019).

Can I start with just one workflow?

Absolutely. Many clients start with one high-impact workflow (lead follow-up, support automation, or report generation) and expand from there. This lets you see ROI quickly and build confidence before scaling.
